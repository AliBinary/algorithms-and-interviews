\textbf{Dijkstra:} \\[1mm]
- Nonnegative edges. \\
- $O(m\log n)$ with heap. \\

\textbf{Bellman-Ford:} \\[1mm]
- Handles negative edges. \\
- $O(nm)$. Detects negative cycles. \\

\textbf{Floyd-Warshall:} \\[1mm]
- All-pairs shortest paths. \\
- $O(n^3)$. \\

\textbf{SPFA:} \\[1mm]
- Optimization of Bellman-Ford, average faster. \\
