\[
\gcd(a,b) = 
\begin{cases}
b & a \bmod b = 0 \\
\gcd(b, a \bmod b) & \text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
\]

Fast exponentiation:
\[
a^b \bmod m =
\begin{cases}
1 & b=0 \\
(a^{b/2})^2 \bmod m & b \text{ even} \\
a \cdot (a^{b-1} \bmod m) & b \text{ odd}
\end{cases}
\]

Sieve of Eratosthenes: find primes up to $n$ in $O(n \log \log n)$.
